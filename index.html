How Digital Literacy Helps Defeat Corruption

In today’s fast-paced online world, corruption has evolved into digital spaces—fake websites, online bribes, manipulation of data, and misinformation campaigns. But with strong digital literacy, citizens become harder to deceive and easier to empower.

When people learn how to detect fake information, protect their data, and analyze online content critically, corruption loses some of its most powerful tools. Technology becomes not just a risk—but a shield.
